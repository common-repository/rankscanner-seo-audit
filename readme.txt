An onsite review of your blog's SEO score, content quality, speed errors, technical warnings and SEO recommendations - and how to fix it all.

== Description ==

Get an automated SEO onsite review of your entire blog, giving you tips and tricks on how to optimise your website for Google and other search engines. The SEO Audit will check everything from content quality, site speed and cache settings, to your HTML markup, inline CSS, duplicated content, various technical issues and much more.

= Onsite SEO Review Features =

*   Find HTML errors on each of your pages
*   Review site speed and suggest fixes
*   Check content quality and identify content issues
*   Suggest optimal cache settings
*   Recommend a wide variety of SEO related optimisations
*   ... and much more!

Create a free account, or use your existing RankScanner account, to get started immediately.

= Technical SEO Audit for your blog =

Request a comprehensive onsite SEO review of your entire blog, and see what you are doing wrong in terms of technical aspects, site speed as well as recommendations for content and other important SEO factors.

= Analyze your onpage SEO factors =

The plugin will go through all of your blog's pages, and let you know when it encounters critical errors or warnings related to factors that Google use to position your site in the organic search results, such as loading speed, readability, HTML structure, inline CSS, misplaced Javascript and much more. Furthermore, you will also receive suggestions for each resource on optimisations and which pages could use some extra content, as the SEO analysis goes through every URL to determine the content quality and length, so it will be easy for you to find out which pages might need some more text content.

Not only will the plugin tell you what is wrong, but also give you detailed information on how exactly you fix each error and warning, so you won't have to spend energy wondering what the best cause of action is. Simply sit back, go through each fix one-by-one and enjoy a perfectly SEO optimised blog when you are done.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Go to 'Plugins' > 'Add New'
2. Search for 'RankScanner: SEO Audit'
3. Activate 'RankScanner: SEO Audit' from your plugins page

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download 'RankScanner: SEO Audit' plugin here or on [RankScanner's official website](https://www.rankscanner.com/wordpress/)
2. Use an FTP client to upload the 'rankscanner-seo-audit' folder to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, or simply go to 'Plugins' > 'Add New' > 'Upload plugin' from your WordPress dashboard and upload the .zip file there
3. Activate 'RankScanner: SEO Audit' from the plugins page

After activating the plugin, all you have to do is to navigate to 'RankScanner' > 'SEO Audit' in the WP sidebar, and you can start reviewing your domain and have the plugin check for onsite errors, warnings and recommendations.

You are now good to go! RankScanner will crawl your blog and give you technical reports of onsite SEO errors, warnings and recommendations for optimisations, whether it\'s for your code, content or server adjustments - all with the purpose of helping your website rank better on Google and other search engines. Enjoy!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How many pages will SEO Audit crawl on my blog? =

The plugin crawls up to 500 pages, depending on which plan you are on.

= Is there any limits on how often I can have my onsite factors reviewed? =

You can request a review as many times as you like, but please allow about an hour to pass in between audits, as RankScanner's engines will need some time to fetch and analyse newly acquired data.
